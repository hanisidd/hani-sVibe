What is Not Pixel Airdrop?

Not Pixel Airdrop is a blockchain-based play-to-earn (P2E) game where users can collect digital assets and participate in various challenges to earn rewards. It's built on the foundation of non-fungible tokens (NFTs) and utilizes crypto tokens for in-game transactions. How to Get Started with Not Pixel Airdrop

Follow these simple steps to start playing Not Pixel Airdrop:

  1. Step 1: Create a Crypto Wallet

    Before diving into the game, ensure you have a crypto wallet like MetaMask. This wallet will store your NFTs and in-game assets. Make sure to have some crypto, such as Ethereum, to pay for small transaction fees.

  2. Step 2: Connect to Not Pixel Airdrop

    Visit the official Not Pixel Airdrop website and connect your crypto wallet to the platform. Ensure your wallet is funded and connected to the correct blockchain network, such as Ethereum or Binance Smart Chain (BSC).

How to Play and Earn in Not Pixel Airdrop

Not Pixel Airdrop offers various gameplay options for earning rewards, such as:

  • Collecting NFTs: Players can collect unique NFTs by participating in the airdrop, which can later be traded or used within the game.
  • Completing Challenges: In-game challenges reward users with tokens and rare NFTs for completing specific tasks or missions.
  • Daily Rewards: Players can claim daily rewards just for logging in and playing.
  • Leaderboard Competitions: Compete against other players to earn higher rankings and claim bigger rewards.
